Butter Chicken

Description

Butter chicken, traditionally known as murgh makhani, an Indian dish which is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture. It originated in the Indian subcontinent as a curry. It is similar to chicken tikka masala, which uses a tomato paste.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Yoghurt
  • Spices

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken with all of the ingredients for the chicken marinade; let marinate for 30 minutes to an hour (or overnight if time allows).
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, chili powder and salt. Let simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally until sauce thickens and becomes a deep brown red colour.
